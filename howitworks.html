How does Artificial Intelligence work?

Thinking and Acting Humanly & Rationally

In 1950, mathematician Alan Turing posed the question, "Can Machines think?" Interested in the human thought-process, Turing inspired the fundamentals of AI. The primary goal of AI is to "simulate human intelligence in machines" by mimicking laws of rational reasoning and human behavior.
