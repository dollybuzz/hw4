How is Artificial Intelligence used?

Narrow AI and Artificial General Intelligence

AI can be broadly understood as two categories: Narrow AI and Artificial General Intelligence.

Narrow AI, or weak AI, is great at performing simple tasks while operating under strict constraints and limitations. Examples of Narrow AI are Google searches, personal assistance devices like Siri or Alexa, and image recognition software.

Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) is the sought after deep-learning that involves human-level intelligence. This practice of AI is the essential goal for many AI researchers. Advancements in AGI are still heavily underway.
